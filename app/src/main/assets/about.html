Test app for Rozetka company

App developed by Alex Karpov

This application is used as HILT dependency injection.

My choice was stopped on this framework, because despite the fact that it is still in the Alpha version, it still works stably, is convenient, and it is also developed by Google and in my opinion it will best fit the Android floor and so will support for a long time and efficiently.

Why didn't I use a clean Dagger 2?

Quite simply, Hilt is an add-on for Dagger 2 and already at this stage all the basic functionality of Dagger 2 is perfectly supported by Hilt.

In this application, I added several additions, such as the history of saving images, as well as share.

Technologies used

  • Hilt (dependency injection)
  • Coil (Loading and show images)
  • Retrofit 2 (Working with API)
  • Room DB (Using for saving history downloaded images)
  • Download manager (Download image)
  • Jetpack navigation (Using for navigation)
